Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI matching work?

MeetAura analyzes your profile — interests, hobbies, skills, habits, and personal notes — and compares them against nearby users to find people who share a similar vibe, even if they use different words to describe the same interests.

How do I enable Auto Detection?

Go to the Auto Detection screen from the home menu and toggle it on. You'll get notified — even with the app closed — whenever someone with a similar vibe enters your selected radius.

What are coins used for?

Coins can be used to unlock extra detection time, generate additional profile drawings, and send offline meeting requests. You can earn coins by watching short rewarded ads in the Coins screen.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

You can block any user directly from their profile page. For serious concerns, please email us at meetaura2026@gmail.com with details and we'll investigate promptly.

How do I delete my account?

Go to setting and scroll down then visible a button name "Delete Account" click on it and your account and data will be deleted.

Why can't I see anyone nearby?

Make sure location permissions are enabled for MeetAura in your device settings, and try increasing your search radius. Also ensure other users in your area have their profiles set to public.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use encrypted connections and Row-Level Security to protect your data. You can also hide specific profile fields and control your visibility at any time from Settings. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
