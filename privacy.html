1. Introduction

MeetAura ("we", "our", or "us") is a social discovery mobile application that helps users find and connect with nearby people who share similar interests. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the MeetAura mobile application (the "App").

By using MeetAura, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

CategoryExamples
Account InformationEmail address, password (encrypted), name
Profile InformationAge, interests, hobbies, habits, music taste, profession, personal notes, avatar/profile picture
Location DataApproximate and precise GPS location, used for nearby matching and detection features
User ContentChat messages, group messages, drawings created in-app, feedback submissions
Usage DataApp interactions, feature usage, in-app coin balance and activity
Device InformationDevice type, operating system, app version, push notification tokens

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match you with nearby users using AI-based compatibility analysis
  • To enable real-time chat, group conversations, and offline meeting requests
  • To send push notifications for detection alerts, messages, and requests
  • To operate our in-app coins and rewarded advertising system
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • To enforce our community guidelines and ensure user safety

4. Location Data

MeetAura's core functionality relies on location data to find nearby users. We collect:

  • Foreground location — while you actively use the Map and Search features
  • Background location — only if you enable Auto Detection mode, so we can notify you when a vibe match enters your radius even when the app is closed
You can disable location access or Auto Detection at any time through your device settings or within the app's Settings screen. Disabling location will limit your ability to use nearby discovery features.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users: Your profile information (excluding hidden fields you've chosen to keep private) is visible to other users for matching purposes
  • Service providers: We use Supabase for backend infrastructure and Google services (Gemini AI, AdMob) to power matching and advertising features
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users

6. Advertising

MeetAura uses Google AdMob to display rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can learn more about Google's advertising practices at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encrypted data transmission and Row-Level Security (RLS) on our database, to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your information, including the ability to:

  • Edit or update your profile information at any time
  • Hide specific profile fields from being visible to other users
  • Disable search visibility or go fully private
  • Block other users
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

9. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. Detection events automatically expire and are deleted after 24 hours. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below, and we will process your request within a reasonable timeframe.

10. Children's Privacy

MeetAura is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a user is underage, we will take steps to delete their account and associated data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
